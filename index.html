Projects:
BarkstaCam

This app is intended to create an internal database for a company to access their user's shared photographs. It acts as a way for the company to have a more personal and collaborative interaction with their users. BarkstaCam was built using React.js, JavaScript, Node.js, Babel, HTML/CSS and Firebase.

Street VNYL
There's no i in VNYL.

A collaborative project that combines music and geolocation! This app allows a user to discover, share and show off music in all the places you visit in New York (and really, anywhere globally!) It's a great way to find what others in the area are listening to and share in their joy that is music. Street VNYL was a collaborative project built using React.js, Node.js, Express, JavaScript, HTML/CSS and the Spotify API and Google Maps API.

Dog Barker

Ever wonder what your dog would post on social media? This is social media for the dogs! They can share their daily barks and howls, complain about their humans and sniff out their friends. Meant to be a fun spoof on tweeting. Dog Barker was built using React.js, Firebase, HTML/CSS and Node.js.
